## 1. Information We Collect
MusicDP does not collect, store, or share personal information from users.

## 2. Permissions Used
The app may request the following permissions only to provide its core functionality:

- **Storage / Media Access**  
  Used to scan and play audio files stored on the user's device.

- **Internet Access**  
  Used to stream audio from URLs provided directly by the user.

The app does not upload or store user media files on any server.

## 3. User-Provided Content
Users may enter URLs to stream audio. The app does not host, control, or distribute any copyrighted content. All content played through URLs is provided by the user.

## 4. Third-Party Services
MusicDP does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## 5. Data Security
Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of personal data exposure through this app.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
MusicDP does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on the app’s listing.
